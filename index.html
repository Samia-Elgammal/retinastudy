Electrical Stimulation for Retinitis Pigmentosa

by Samia Elgammal

Abstract Research Paper Poster Figshare

ðŸ“˜ Abstract

Retinitis Pigmentosa (RP) leads to progressive vision loss due to the degeneration of retinal cells. Current treatments are limited, and while retinal implants show promise, they are complex and invasive. This study proposes a wearable, non-invasive visual prosthetic that uses targeted electrical stimulation to restore vision. Initial in-vitro tests showed encouraging cellular responses. Future work will include in-vivo testing and refinement.
